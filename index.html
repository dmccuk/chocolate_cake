Recipe

Chocolate cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"

DifficultyPreperationCookingTotal Time
Easy10 min25 Min35 min

Ingredients

Dark chocolate: 200g

Sugar : 150g

Butter : 200g

Eggs : 4

Flour : 80g

Instructions

1

Melt the cocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugare eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
